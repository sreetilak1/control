  • TOP FOUR MOVIES
    1. KANGUVA

      Kanguva" is a Tamil action-adventure film directed by Siva, starring Suriya, Bobby Deol, and Disha Patani. The movie follows a bounty hunter in the present day who is mysteriously linked to a tribal warrior from a millennium ago, as they both struggle to protect their people and seek justice. The film explores themes of reincarnation and betrayal, with a prophecy at its core.

    2. MAHARAJA

      Maharaja" is a 2024 Indian Tamil-language action thriller film directed by Nithilan Saminathan and starring Vijay Sethupathi, Anurag Kashyap, and Mamta Mohandas. The film follows a barber in Chennai who goes to the police station to retrieve a stolen dustbin, but the police discover his intentions are more complex. The movie premiered on June 12, 2024, and was theatrically released on June 14, 2024, according to Wikipedia.

    3. AMARAN

      "Amaran" is a 2024 Tamil biographical drama film starring Sivakarthikeyan and Sai Pallavi, telling the story of Major Mukund Varadarajan, a martyred Indian Army officer, and his love story with his wife Indhu. The film, directed by Rajkumar Periasamy, explores themes of courage, loyalty, sacrifice, and patriotism, set against the backdrop of counter-terrorism operations in Kashmir

    4. BLUE STAR

      "Blue Star" is a sports drama focusing on the rivalry between two cricket teams from different castes in a Tamil village. The film explores themes of caste discrimination, social inequality, and the power of sports beyond the field. The story follows the journey of Ranjith (Ashok Selvan), a Dalit cricket enthusiast, and his team Blue Star, as they confront their arch-rivals, Alpha, and their own societal barriers. The film also delves into the personal stories and struggles of the characters involved, highlighting the impact of caste on their lives and aspirations.
